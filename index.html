ABOUT ME

15 years worked in 3D software. I have worked in the video game industry at Nemosoft, then Mekensleep before joigning OutFlop where I have lead the 3D client technology. I have worked as freelance around OpenSceneGraph technology, I am also the authors and maintainer of osgAnimation in OpenSceneGraph. I am the CTO of Sketchfab, which I co-founded in 2012.
